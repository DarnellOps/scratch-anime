My Hero Academia

As of now, My Hero Academia is my favorite anime of all time. I love it. I don't know, something about having powers has always resonated with me. Watching the protagonist Izuku Midoriya (hero name Deku), transform from this timid little boy to powerhouse UA student is so enjoyable. I love everything about MHA, there isn't anything in it I dislike. I like all the students, I want them all to succeed.

I watched the first season because I saw positive sentiment on the internet. It's an action shonen anime which is right up my alley. I wasn't crazy about it but I enjoyed the first season, it was interesting. But the second season is what got me hooked on the show, from there, the show got better and better. I started to care for the characters, cheering them on, wanting them to achieve their goals. I wanted to be there with them, that's how much I like the characters.

I've collected up to season 3 of the blu ray releases, up to 25 manga volumes and the first movie while I was still working. When I became unemployed, I had to stop buying things here and there for my favorite franchises. When I do get back on my feet, I'll be sure to bolster my collection with season 4 and 5 as well as manga volumes up to 30. I'd say my merch between DBZ and MHA is comparable in size but MHA will definitely eclipse Dragon Ball at some point.
