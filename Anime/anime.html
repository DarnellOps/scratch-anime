Anime


I really love anime. My first experience with anime were on Saturday mornings with Kids' WB on channel 39 and Fox Kids on channel 26 in Houston back in the late 90s and early 2000s. The shows I liked watching were Pokemon, YuGiOh, Sailor Moon, Cardcaptor Sakura and more. I didn't know they were considered anime until much later. I thought they were your typical American cartoon that every kid liked waking up early to watch. I had no idea they were made in Japan and were then dubbed for American audiences. As I grew older, I learned that most anime are adaptations of Japanese comics called manga. The biggest of which is called Shonen Jump where some of the most popular series originated.

Since watching many different anime over the years, I think I've found my top 3 favorite anime. My Hero Academia, Naruto, and Dragon Ball Z. These are by no means what I consider masterpieces, I can make another post about that, these are just what I enjoy immensely while watching. I just get into such a good mood when I get to sit down I watch them. I'll give each there own page.
